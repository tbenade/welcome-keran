Who

Keran McKenzie - MYOB API Evangelist hot off the stage from APIDAYS, has kindly accepted to come share with SEEK the importance of an API and the associated community.

Keran’s always been a tinkerer, someone who constantly looks for ways to change things up and do things differently. Over the past 20 years Keran has worked in education, enterprise and has founded and run a number of startups. He loves the business journey and his role at MYOB sees him enjoying the best of an enterprise company while spending many of his days immersed in startups.
